About

PetPal is an interactive web app that connects future pet owners with their ideal furry (or feathered!) companion and provides a personalized care guide to ensure a happy, healthy life together. Whether you're a first-time adopter or an experienced pet parent, we make finding — and caring for — your perfect match simple and fun.

Our Mission

We believe every pet deserves a loving home and every adopter deserves the tools to care for their new companion with confidence. Our mission is to improve pet adoption success rates by matching people with pets that fit their lifestyle and providing tailored guidance to help them thrive from day one.

How it Works

  1. Take the Quiz — Answer a few quick questions about your lifestyle, home, and preferences.
  2. Get Your Match — We'll suggest a pet profile that fits you best. Ties are handled with random selection between your top matches.
  3. Receive Your Care Guide — Get a personalized guide covering nutrition, exercise, grooming, and health tips for your new friend.
